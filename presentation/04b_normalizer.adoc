* The LLM says `$1,500.00 USD` one time and `1500 usd` the next
* Different strings? Yes. Same invoice? Yes.

==== Normalization Rules (Pure Java, No LLM)

[cols="1,2,2"]
|===
| Field | Before | After

| Date | `"Jan 15, 2024"` | `2024-01-15`
| Amount | `$1,500.00` | `1500.00`
| Currency | `usd` | `USD`
| Strings | `"  INV-001  "` | `INV-001`
|===
