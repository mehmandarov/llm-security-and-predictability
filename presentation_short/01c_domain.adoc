=== The Domain — Invoice Extraction

---

```
Raw Invoice Text  ──►  LLM  ──►  ExtractedInvoice (JSON)
                                   ├── invoiceNumber
                                   ├── date
                                   ├── amount
                                   ├── currency
                                   ├── vendorName
                                   └── items[]
```

==== Why Invoices?

* Boring on purpose — every department has them
* Real money, real audit trails
* If we can't trust the LLM with an invoice, we can't trust it with a contract or a customer record

_Pick one workflow. Make it bullet-proof. Then expand._
