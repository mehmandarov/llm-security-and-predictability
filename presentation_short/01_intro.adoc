=== Introduction
==== The Promise vs. The Reality

---

* "Who has used an LLM at work in the last week? Keep your hand up if you'd let it sign a contract on your behalf."
* LLMs are *probabilistic*. Business logic is not.
* The question isn't whether the model will be wrong. *It will be wrong.*
The question is what your system does when it is.

====
*Promise of this talk:* in 20 minutes you'll have a vocabulary and a mental model for shipping LLM features that don't end up in a post-mortem.
