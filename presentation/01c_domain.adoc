=== The Domain — Invoice Extraction

---

```
Raw Invoice Text  ──►  LLM  ──►  ExtractedInvoice (JSON)
                                  ├── invoiceNumber
                                  ├── date
                                  ├── amount
                                  ├── currency
                                  ├── vendorName
                                  └── items[]
                                       ├── description
                                       ├── quantity
                                       └── unitPrice
```

==== Why Invoices?

* Simple enough to fit in your head
* Complex enough to hallucinate (dates, math, currencies)
* Real-world use case — every company has one

_One domain. Five layers of defense. The chaos comes from the model, not the problem._
