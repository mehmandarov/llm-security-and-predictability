=== Takeaway — Three Lines

---

[.lead]
1. *LLMs are not the application.* +
They are _one component_ in the application — the unreliable one.

[.lead]
2. *Wrap the unreliable component.* +
Guardrails on the way in. Validators on the way out. A *typed verdict* for the caller.

[.lead]
3. *Make failure boring.* +
A flagged invoice is a routine workflow. A silent failure in production is a Sunday-night incident.

---

[NOTE]
====
*"We're not here to make LLMs perfect. We're here to make systems that fail safely and predictably when they're not. That's the boring, profitable engineering this technology actually needs."*
====
